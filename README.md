Common Types of Small Roofing Repairs

Even small roofs need attention. Typical issues homeowners face include:

Shingle damage – curling, cracking, or missing shingles

Leaks around vents and chimneys – often caused by worn flashing

Gutter problems – blocked gutters can cause water damage

Roof decking issues – soft or weakened wood under shingles

Addressing these issues promptly can prevent water damage, mold growth, and costly repairs.

Signs You Need a Roofing Repair

Some signs that you may need roofing repairs include:

Water stains on ceilings or walls

Shingles that are loose or missing

Granules accumulating in gutters

Sudden spikes in energy bills
